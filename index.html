About Me

I'm Lene. Solar powered and live-loving seadipper, adventurer, Co-Founder and story-teller of Dawnstalkers.

I moved to South Wales during lockdown in August 2020, found myself in Penarth, started a movement of like-minded people who were longing for social interaction and connection and decided to stay in this magical place.

What I care about

Everything about human connection and inspiring each other. I thrive of really getting to know the people I surround myself with.

Nature is a healer and I'm grateful for being able to reconnect with myself in the sea every morning while watching the sunrise.
